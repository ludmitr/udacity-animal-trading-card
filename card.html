Brown Bear

Brown bear can smell food from up to 20 miles away and can detect the scent of carrion from more than a mile away.

  • Scientific Name: Brown bear
  • Average Length: 8 feet(2.4 meters)
  • Average Lifespan: 20-25 years
  • Habital: Northern Hemisphere

Brown bears are one of the largest carnivorous mammals found in North America, Europe, and Asia. They are known for their distinctive brown fur and impressive physical strength. While they are adaptable to a range of environments, brown bears require access to food, shelter, and water to survive.
